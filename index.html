WHAT IS

Skikda
i-Tech
Club?

A scientific club interested in new technologies. It is aimed at all students of the university August 20, 1955 with their different specialties. I-tech is a place for meeting and exchange between “beginners or professionals” students who wish to improve their knowledge in the field of IT and new technologies and also develop their skills in different areas. The main goal of this club is to promote, in the interest of our members, the use of tools and technologies and to learn new languages in programming and to develop the personal side.

The Value-added

Our club is the only club at the university specialized in the field of computer science. It aims to assist students and provide them with opportunities to learn more about the field, especially computer science students. We prepare them for practical life.
